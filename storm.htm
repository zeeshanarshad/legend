Bringer of Storm

He is the One who makes you see the lightening in fear and hope, and who forms the heavy clouds. (Legendary Quran 13.12)

Allah controls the wind, storms and lightning. He throws lightning wherever he wants on whomever he wants. He destroys the nations by taking his armies of storms wherever he wants. When Allah intends to do something, then there is no stopping His intention and Allah does whatever He wills. The only ruler and sole king of the kingdom of the entire universe is Allah alone.
